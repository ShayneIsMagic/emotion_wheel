# Comprehensive Emotional Assessment System

## Overview
This assessment combines validated psychological instruments including the Geneva Emotion Wheel (GEW), PANAS scales, and dimensional emotion models to provide a comprehensive understanding of your emotional experience. The system tracks your progress over time and provides insights into emotional patterns and trends.

---

## Assessment Instructions

### Time Required
- **Initial Assessment**: 15-20 minutes
- **Follow-up Assessments**: 10-15 minutes
- **PDF Upload & Analysis**: 2-3 minutes

### Privacy & Data
- All responses are confidential and stored locally
- No personal data is transmitted to external servers
- Assessment results are saved with timestamps for trend analysis

## Research Foundation

### Assessment Instruments
- **Geneva Emotion Wheel (GEW)**: Validated across 9+ languages, test-retest reliability r > .70
- **PANAS**: Internal consistency α = .85-.90 (PA), .84-.87 (NA)
- **Circumplex Model**: Empirically supported dimensional structure

### Clinical Applications
- Treatment outcome monitoring
- Emotion regulation therapy
- Cross-cultural emotion research
- Psychiatric assessment adjunct

### Citations
- Geneva Emotion Wheel: Scherer et al. (2013)
- PANAS: Watson, Clark & Tellegen (1988)
- Circumplex Model: Russell (1980)
